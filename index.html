Intro

In 2003, I successfully completed a BA (Hons) in Jewellery and Silversmithing and a HND in Design Crafts.

I now have my own workshop, where I have been designing and making jewellery for the past 20 years. I have sold my jewellery in the gallery at the Pavillion Gardens, where I was part of the High Peak Artists and Designers Consortium. My jewellery has also been exhibited in local gallerys and shops. I currently exhibit my work at the Green Man Gallery in Buxton.

Using the knowledge and practical skills learnt on my degree, I am able to use and practice a wide range of techniques to design and make unique bespoke jewellery. I have undertaken commissions where I worked closely with the client to design and make a unique piece; ranging from a small brooch to a large sculpture for the garden. If you'd like to see some examples of my work, please take a look at my gallery.

I take inspiration from the surrounding nature and architecture to explore form, texture and pattern; incorporating them in my designs to create contrast in both material and shape.

Using a mixture of recycled materials, old silver cutlery, broken watches and discarded jewellery or a small pebble or shell that I've found upon the shore line. I've even used the wood from the log store to use in my work. I enjoy working with old unwanted materials and turning them into something beautiful, I enjoy manipulating the material to bring them back to life by creating a unique piece of jewellery.

Weddings

Let's talk weddings!!

I make bespoke wedding bands in various metals, each designed and made for the special couple. Whether it's a high polish or a brushed Matt, everything down to the final detail is finalised with the client. It's very special to me, knowing I'm making something so precious that symbolising so much - Love, commitment and life-long promises.

Each ring I make is presented in a hand made personalised box. Again, I make this specially and specifically for each couple by using their initials, special date, first dance lyrics, ect.

I also make wedding table decorations (jars), invitations, place-names and favours for the guests (baubles).
